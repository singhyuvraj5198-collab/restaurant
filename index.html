Our Story

A Legacy of
Culinary Mastery

Founded in 1998 by Chef Antoine Moreau, Élysée represents the pinnacle of gastronomic excellence. Every dish is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, creativity, and the timeless art of French cuisine.

We source the finest ingredients from artisan producers worldwide, transforming them into extraordinary culinary experiences that engage all five senses. Our philosophy is simple — respect the ingredient, honor the tradition, surprise the palate.

Antoine Moreau

Executive Chef & Founder
